About Us

Keas Village Student Accommodation provides safe, clean, and affordable housing for students in Kimberley. Our residence is designed to support academic success with a peaceful and secure living environment.

Our Accommodation Locations

Keas Village Student Accommodation operates across multiple student residences in Kimberley. This allows us to accommodate more students while maintaining a safe, structured, and well-managed living environment.

  • ✔ 2 to 3 separate student houses
  • ✔ All houses managed under Keas Village
  • ✔ Same rules, pricing, and management standards
  • ✔ Students are allocated based on availability

Exact house placement is confirmed after booking, depending on room availability and student preference.

NSFAS Information

Keas Village Student Accommodation is suitable for students funded by NSFAS. We understand the needs of funded students and aim to provide affordable, safe, and stable accommodation.

  • ✔ Proof of residence available
  • ✔ Monthly rental structure
  • ✔ Receipts provided upon payment
  • ✔ Student-focused living environment

Note: NSFAS payments and accommodation arrangements are handled in accordance with agreed terms between the student and management.

House Rules & Policies

  • ✔ Accommodation is strictly for registered students
  • ✔ Quiet hours must be respected for study purposes
  • ✔ No overcrowding beyond agreed room capacity
  • ✔ Visitors allowed during approved hours only
  • ✔ Rooms and common areas must be kept clean
  • ✔ Any damages will be charged to the occupant
  • ✔ Monthly rent must be paid as agreed

These rules are in place to ensure a safe, respectful, and productive living environment for all residents.
